## About CamFinTech

CamFinTech is a platform provider that enables **policy-driven automation across all industries** through the **Cambodia General Accepted Practices (GaaP) compliance framework**.

Our 8-layer GaaP framework provides:
1. **Identity** - Secure, verified identity across all interactions
2. **Interoperability** - Seamless cross-system integration
3. **Payments** - Compliant financial transactions (where applicable)
4. **Compliance & Audit** - Immutable audit trails and regulatory compliance
5. **Localization** - Multi-language, multi-region support
6. **Sectoral** - Industry-specific regulations and policies
7. **Applications** - Workflow automation and orchestration
8. **Analytics** - Insights, optimization, and continuous improvement
